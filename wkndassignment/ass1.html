Sweet iced Cofee with Coconut Milk
sweet iced cofee with coconut milk is easy to make and stores well in the fridge

    Instructions
  1. Boil some water and set it for 1-2 minutes
  2. put the cofee grounds into a cafeteire and pour in the water
  3. let the cofee steep for five minutes then slowly press down the plunger into the cafeteire
  4. pour the cofee into a jug allow it to cool then chill for several hours
  5. whisk in the coconut milk and condensed milk and serve over plenty of ice
  6. this cofee can be stored in the fridge for up to five days.Shake or stir again before sirving
